Instructions:

  • Navigate to "Play"
  • Click "Start Game"
  • A randomly generated color swatch will show on the window, guess each R, G, and B value, either by a slider, or a 2-digit hex value
  • By default, you will have 3 guesses. Configure number of guesses from 1-5 in Settings if you'd like, you can also change you user name!
  • After each guess, the percentage-off for each R, G, and B will be displayed for improvements, score will be updated.
  • You will have 2 minutes for each game, pay attention to the timer and number of guesses left
  • When timer reaches maxinum or number of guesses run out, game is finished and you'll see your score summary
  • Click High Score to check out your the best!
