Instructions:

  • Navigate to "Play"
  • Click "New Game"
  • A randomly generated color swatch will show on the window, guess each R, G, and B value, either by a slider, or a 2-digit hex value
  • By default, you will have 3 guesses. Confugure number of guesses from 1-5 in Settings if you'd like
  • After each guess, the percentage-off for each R, G, and B will be displayed for improvements. Score will be updated based on performance
  • After each guess, press Continue to resume, timer will be stopped until Continue is pressed
  • You will have 2 minutes for each game, pay attention to the timer and number of guesses left
  • When timer or number of guesses run out, game is finished and you'll see your score summary and answer
